Art and Antique furniture

Preserving and protecting art and antique furniture involves careful storage and insurance. These pieces are often delicate, valuable, and irreplaceable, requiring specialized care. We choose the right environment for storage of Special furniture Temperature & Humidity: Maintain a stable environment with temperatures around 65–75°F (18–24°C) and humidity levels between 40–60%. Avoid drastic fluctuations that can cause cracking or warping. Lighting: Keep items in low or no direct light. UV rays can cause fading, discoloration, and degradation of materials. Ventilation: Ensure good airflow to prevent mold or mildew growth.

Packing with extra care

We wrap furniture in breathable materials like muslin or acid-free sheets to shield it from dust and dirt. Avoid using plastic as it can trap moisture.
