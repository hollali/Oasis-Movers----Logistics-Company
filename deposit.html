1.200 mq Deposit

At Oasis, we require a deposit to secure your moving or storage services. The deposit ensures that your booking is confirmed and that we can prepare the necessary resources for your move. The amount is typically a small percentage of the total cost, depending on the service being provided. Deposits are refundable under certain conditions, and the balance is due on the day of the move or when the service is completed. Our goal is to offer transparent and flexible payment options to make your experience with us as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Preparing

Packing and labeling
  • Label boxes on all sides so you can see what it contains in a stack without having to move everything around.
